Human
Humans are the most adaptable and ambitious people among the common races. Individually and as a group, humans are adaptable opportunists, and they stay alert to changing political and social dynamics.
No attribute modifiers

Elf
Elves are a long-lived humanoid race, more slender and agile than humans. They can be distinguished by their large, keen eyes and their pointed ears. They have a close kinship with both nature and magic.
Dexterity +1, Intelligence +2
Strength -2, Stamina -1

Dwarf
The dwarves are an old race, as old as the earth itself. They are stubborn and set in their ways, but only because their ways have historically worked well for them. They are usually miners, blacksmiths and warriors.
Strength +2, Stamina +1
Dexterity -2, Intelligence -1

Dark Elf
Dark Elves are one of the most ancient, powerful, and certainly the most deadly living race. They live in underground cities and they are do anything to protect their secrets. They are great spies and assassins.
Dexterity +2, Stamina +1
Strength -1, Intelligence -2

Warrior
Warriors put emphasis on strength and physical combat. Wielding melee weapons, they typically lack range, or have only limited ranged attack options, but they excel at fighting enemies in close range combat, often having high HP, defense, and attack capabilities to aid in this.
Strength +2, Stamina +1
Intelligence -2, Dexterity -1

Ranger
Rangers are usually associated with the wisdom of nature. Rangers spend a great deal of time hunting, fishing, and camping - whether on a short- or long-term basis. They prefer ranged weapons.
Dexterity +2, Stamina +1
Strength -2, Intelligence -1

Mage
Mages are a "genius students" of arcane magic, who have studied the subject for years. They are considered to be spellcasters who wield powerful spells, but are often physically weak as a trade-off.
Stamina +1, Intelligence +2
Strength -2, Dexterity -1

Assassin
Assassins typically combine combat ability with strong stealth skills, and specialise in defeating an enemy without becoming involved in a protracted melee. They are using weapons that are small, lightweight, and often concealable.
Strength +1, Dexterity +2
Stamina -1, Intelligence -2
